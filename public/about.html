What is Digital Table?

Digital Table is a platform designed to make playing Tabletop Role Playing Games over the internet easy!

What are Tabletop Role Playing Games?

You may recognize names like "Dungeons and Dragons" or "Pathfinder". These games are examples of tabletop rpgs. I am way too lazy to explain this right now. Go google it.

Do I have to pay?

Nope! Digital Table is a free service. That being said, there are some perks to becoming a donating member of the Digital Table community. These are all non-essential perks, such as being able to run more games simultaneously, and having extra character sheet templates.
